Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Jasson Farrier, Realtor · Kiger Realty

Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit JassonFarrier.com or interact with our services. Jasson Farrier is an independent real estate agent with Kiger Realty. This policy does not apply to the privacy practices of Kiger Realty or other independent agents.

Information We Collect

When you contact us: When you submit a form, request our free guide, or reach out with questions, we collect the information you provide — including your name, email, and phone number. We may add you to our email list for housing market updates. You can opt out at any time.

Information collected automatically: Our website automatically collects certain technical information including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing patterns. We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide real estate services
  • To deliver housing market content and updates via email (with your consent)
  • To maintain the security and functionality of our website
  • For research, analysis, and service improvement
  • To comply with legal obligations

How We Share Your Information

Service providers: We may share information with providers who help deliver our services, such as email platforms and website hosting. These providers are authorized to use your information only as necessary to perform services on our behalf.

Legal disclosures: We may share information as required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or to prevent fraud or illegal activity.

We do not sell your personal information.

Your Rights and Choices

You may opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email. We will continue to send important administrative messages as needed. Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. Contact us at 614-699-3007 to exercise these rights.

Information Security

We maintain security measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No data transmission or storage system is 100% secure.

Children

Links to Other Websites
