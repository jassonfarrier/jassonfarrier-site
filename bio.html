I'm a real estate agent and investor based in Central Ohio, focused on helping buyers and sellers navigate today's housing market with clear, data-driven guidance.

In addition to working directly with clients, I publish regular housing market analysis on my YouTube channels — Ohio Housing Nerd and Housing Nerd — where thousands follow my breakdowns of inventory trends, price shifts, and what's really happening in the market. My philosophy is simple: educate first, advise second. No pressure, no fluff.

"I'm not here to sell you on buying a home. I'm here to make sure you understand what you're walking into."

Before real estate, I earned my MBA and led a national sales team in the construction safety industry. That experience shaped how I work today — direct communication, strong preparation, and strategy over sales tactics.

I actively invest in real estate myself — buy-and-hold rentals across Central Ohio. So when I analyze this market, I'm not doing it from a distance. I have skin in the game every single day.
